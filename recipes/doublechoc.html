Double Chocolate Chip Recipe

Description

I asked a friend once "If I can make any cookie, what would you like?". This is why i decided to learn this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/8 cups of butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Cream butter, sugar then add eggs and vanilla in a stand mixer.
  2. Mix in cocoa and add baking soda, salt to the wet mixture.
  3. Add flour and chocolate chips to the mixture and mix.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 8-9 minutes.
