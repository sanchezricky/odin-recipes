Breakfast Sausage

Description

I have always wanted to create a leaner version of my favorite sausage, the breakfast sausage. I use pork loin for my "meat" and bacon for my "fat". I have been making it like this for years and everyone loves it. It tastes the same but without it being so greasy.

I have always used the Alton Brown recipe for breakfast sausage. Growing up, I always admired the way Alton Brown would teach in his shows.

The recipe is very simple and does not require fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds pork loin
  • 1/2 pound bacon, or more if you'd like
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons freshly ground pepper
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh sage leaves
  • 2 teaspoons finely chopped fresh thyme leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon finely chopped rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/2 grated nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Combine spices and herbs with cut pork. Chill for 1 hour.
  2. Run mixture through a fine blade meat grinder.
  3. Form 1 inch rounds or little fingies.
  4. Cook for 10-15 minutes and enjoy!
