Carnitas Recipe

Description

Carnitas is my favorite Mexican meat and thats it.

For this recipe I use a dutch oven.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds boneless pork shoulder
  • Sea salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 jalepenos
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 1 orange
  • 4 limes
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 teaspoon chile powder
  • 2-4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 red onions
  • Cilantro

Instructions

  1. Cut pork shoulder into 4 separate pieces. This helps cook the pork faster.
  2. Salt liberally.
  3. Add olive oil to the dutch oven and cook on medium heat. Once the oil thins add pork to the dutch oven. Brown on all sides and remove from dutch oven.
  4. Add onions, jalepenos and cook for 2 minutes. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add in the juice of the oranges and orange peel. Add lime juice.
  6. Add pork into the pan.
  7. Add in enough broth to cover 2/3 of the pork. Add cinnamon stick and chile powder.
  8. Cover and braise for 5 hours or until the meat is easy to shred.
  9. Shred pork and strain the liquid into a separate container.
  10. Place pork on a baking sheet and add 1 cup of the reserves broth.
  11. Cook pork in oven at 375 until it gets crunchy!
