Gyoza Recipe

Description

I originally was inspired to learn how to make gyozas from reading a book I enjoyed. After my first attempt I realized how easy and delicious these were.

I love making these because they have a great balance of macro nutrients and a good amount of fiber

Gyoza wrapper ingredients

  • 2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Hot Water

Instructions for the gyoza wrapper

  1. In a bow sift the flour and salt and make a valley in the center.
  2. Add the hot water to the center of the valley and start mixing. I use a fork to get things moving
  3. Add the dough to a well floured surface and knead for 10 minutes.
  4. Place kneaded dough in a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap and let sit for at least 2 hours
